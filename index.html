What We Do

End-to-End Design & QS Services

From initial concept to final takeoff — every construction design and quantity surveying service you need under one roof, delivered with precision across the UAE.

Structural Design

Analysis and design of RCC, steel, and composite structures using ETABS, SAFE, and STAAD.Pro — compliant with Dubai Municipality, Abu Dhabi, and UAE building codes.

Rebar Shop Drawings

Cost Analysis

Architecture Design

Interior Design

How We Work

From Enquiry to Delivery

Our streamlined workflow ensures your UAE construction project stays on track, on budget, and on schedule.

Step 01

Enquiry & Scope

Share your project requirements with us. We review the available information and define a clear scope of work tailored to your UAE construction project.

Step 02

Technical Review

Our team assesses project requirements, identifies key considerations for Dubai, Abu Dhabi, or Sharjah regulations, and recommends the most effective approach.

Step 03

Execution

We carry out the required design or detailing services with regular progress updates, coordination, and review milestones to ensure quality outcomes.
